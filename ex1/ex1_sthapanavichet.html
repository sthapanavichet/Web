Spider-Man

character-info

Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, is a fictional superhero created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. Possessing spider-like abilities gained from a radioactive spider bite, Spider-Man swings into action to protect the people of New York City. With his agility, strength, and iconic web-shooters, Spider-Man faces various villains while balancing the challenges of a dual life as Peter Parker.
